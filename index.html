Family-Owned.

Kapoor Traders has been serving the people of Shikohabad and surrounding areas for over 35 years. What started as a small roadside shop has grown into the region's go-to destination for quality tyres and auto parts.

We believe in honest pricing, genuine products, and treating every customer like family. Whether you drive a hatchback, SUV, tractor, truck or any two-wheeler — we've got you covered.
